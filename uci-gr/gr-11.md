### Variational Principle

$$\begin{align*}
I_E &= \frac{1}{16\pi G}\int\,d^4x\,\sqrt{g}\cdot R\quad (\text{Einstein-Hilbert action})\\
\delta I_E &= 0\\
\end{align*}$$
